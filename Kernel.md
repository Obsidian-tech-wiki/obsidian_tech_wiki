A **kernel** is a key part of a [[Operating system|operating system]] and plays the important role of controlling the [[Hardware|hardware]] and [[Software|software]].
![[Kernel diagram.png]]
## Types
There are five types of kernels:
- A micro kernel
- A monolithic kernel
- A hybrid kernel
- Exokernel
- Nanokernel
