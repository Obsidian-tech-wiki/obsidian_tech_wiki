**macOS** is a [[UNIX]]-based [[Wiki/Software/Operating systems/Operating system|operating system]] developed by [[Apple]] for use on their [[Macintosh]] line of computers. macOS is [[proprietary]] [[Software|software]] meaning that the code is not open sourced.
